Open-source projects thrive on collaboration. By contributing to these projects, you can not only learn new skills but also earn rewards through bounty programs.

Some platforms that host bounties include Gitcoin, Bugcrowd, and HackerOne, where you can contribute to projects or find vulnerabilities and get paid for your efforts.
